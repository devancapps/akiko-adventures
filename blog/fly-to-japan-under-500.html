Find Cheap Flights to Japan

Introduction

Japan is a dream destination for many travelers, but the cost of flights can be a major barrier. In this guide, we'll show you how to find flights to Japan for under $500, making your dream trip more affordable than ever.

Best Time to Book

The key to finding cheap flights to Japan is timing. Here are the best periods to book:

  • January to March (excluding New Year's)
  • September to November
  • Mid-week flights (Tuesday to Thursday)

Top Airlines for Budget Flights

Several airlines regularly offer deals to Japan:

  • AirAsia
  • Jetstar
  • Peach Aviation
  • Zipair Tokyo

Booking Tips

Follow these tips to secure the best deals:

  1. Set up price alerts on Trip.com
  2. Be flexible with your travel dates
  3. Consider alternative airports
  4. Book at least 3-4 months in advance
